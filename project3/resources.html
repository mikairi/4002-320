Content on web site

  • The arrow on the menu box that points to the current page was made from scratch.
  • YouTube videos are embedded on the Music Videos page.
  • A Site map was created as a secondary navigation system.
  • The banner of the site has a rollover effect.

Extra Work

  • JavaScript scripts were written to show/hide second-level menu items on the menu box, as well as to show/hide track lists on the Discography page. A script was written to "stick" the menu box to the window when user scrolls past it.
  • CSS3 property box-shadow is used on the menu box.

Reflection

I feel the site was well designed. What I think I did the best in is that the contrast/color of the site goes easy on the eye. I also made sure that the navigation system is consistent and always available to the user. It also stands out from the rest of the page.

What I might have did poorly on is the amount of content on the site. Since the band is relatively new, there is not much background information about them. However, I focused on their musical activities/resources so that new fans or listeners can find out about songs that they missed.
