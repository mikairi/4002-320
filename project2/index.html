APink

APink (에이핑크) is a seven-member girl group from South Korea. They debuted on April 21, 2011, with their single "I Don't Know" (몰라요). Since then, they have released two EPs and one full studio album.

Biography

The group consists of leader Park Cho Rong, Yoon Bo Mi, Jeong Eun Ji, Son Na Eun, Hong Yoo Kyung, Kim Nam Joo, and Oh Ha Young.

On February 11, 2011, Son Na Eun was the first member to be revealed, although she had appeared earlier in the music video for "Beautiful" by Beast, a label-mate boyband. The next member, Park Cho Rong, was revealed a few days later. On the 21st, Oh Ha Young was revealed on Twitter. Jeong Eun Ji and Hong Yoo Kyung were introduced on March 10. Yoon Bo Mi and Kim Nam Joo were the last to be revealed, along with the full group.
